Engagement

As the situation with Covid-19 was deteriorating while Roman and Karen were in Vancouver, we decided to head back to Toronto at the beginning of April. On our way through BC, Roman asked Karen to stop to look at the beautiful mountains in Yoho National Park. Then he pulled out a cute note and a story he had written, which was Karen's life re-imagined as a fairytale involving many dragons.

And he asked her to marry him!

As Roman says, important questions should be asked in the middle of nowhere. And his friend Jaron astutely points out, this reduces the complexity of the problem, leaving only the most essential features, and the cold snowy setting helps reduce the thermal population of the excited states as well. Very important for getting a clear measurement result.

And she said yes!
